About Us

WHO WE ARE

Plastiki champions is a youth led community based environmental non government organization with children and youth spear heading the fight against plastic and organic waste pollution for a waste free tomorrow. Plastiki champions seek to educate, inspire and empower children and youth to drive action in creating awareness to strengthen the waste management sector and enable waste policy negotiations in Uganda. Plastiki champions seek to combat the surge in plastic pollution and promote a culture of sustainability and environmental stewardship.

Our Mission

To educate, inspire and empower young people and communities to adopt zero waste practices, reducing waste and promoting sustainable living

Our story

Plastiki Champions was founded in September, 2024 by Miss Namwanga Patricia Kizito, driven by passion to address the devastating impacts of poor waste management which had recently led to loss of lives; livestock and property in a land fill disaster in Uganda. Since then we are growing into a vibrant community of young change makers working together to:

Educate and raise

Educate and raise awareness about waste management..

Developing solutions

Developing innovative solutions for reducing waste.

Supporting policy changes

Supporting policy changes and advocacy efforts for a waste free tomorrow.

Empowering young people

Empowering young people and communities to take action.
